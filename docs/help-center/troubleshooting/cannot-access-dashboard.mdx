---
title: Cannot Access Dashboard
description: Resolve login issues on Anesthesia SmartSite.
---

## Symptoms
- Login page reloads
- Access denied message

## Causes
- Incorrect credentials
- Browser cache issues
- Account not activated

## Solution
<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify credentials">
    Double-check your email and password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear cache">
    Logout, then clear browser cache and cookies.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Resend activation">
    If still locked out, click "Resend activation link" or contact support.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact Support">
    If access is still denied, reach out to our support team for assistance.
  </Step>
</Steps>
