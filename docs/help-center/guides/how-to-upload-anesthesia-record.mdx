## Overview
Upload scanned anesthesia records directly to your SmartSite dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in">
    Navigate to the SmartSite dashboard and log in to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to 'Records'">
    Click the 'Records' tab in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload file">
    Click 'Upload', select your file, then confirm upload.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm submission">
    Wait for the confirmation message to ensure the record was submitted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify upload">
    Check that the file appears in your records list.
  </Step>
</Steps>
