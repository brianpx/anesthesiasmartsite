## Overview

Anesthesia SmartSite is an online resource by Brian Pitts, MD designed for anesthesia professionals. 

## Key Features
- Centralized document management
- Secure anesthesia record uploads
- Guides and quick tips for clinical workflows
- Access to updates and changelogs

## Who Can Use SmartSite?
Anyone looking to streamline anesthesia documentation, access resources, and connect with Dr. Pitts' materials.
